Services
I offer technical support to businesses in selecting, transforming and recycling thermoplastics.

Thermoplastics - Selection:

Help in selecting the polymer, grade, and processing technique based on customer requirements

  • Mechanical performance
  • Thermal resistance
  • Chemical resistance
  • Long-terme stability
  • Food contact
  • Cost

  • Participation in the development of a specification
  • Contacts with suppliers
  • Assistance with the final selection

  • Maximization of productivity
